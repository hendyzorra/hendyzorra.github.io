THE STUDY PROGRAM OF
INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The under graduate studies in Computer Science is designed to equip the graduates with the ability to have :

1. Integrity and identity as an Indonesian

2. Computer science and communication skill

3. Computer science and communication observation

4. Social and cultural intellectuality

5. Design and Web-design skill

6. Continuing study


In general the available careers for the under graduates studies in Computer Science are :

1. Programmer

2. System Analyst

3. System Programmer

4. Software Engineer

5. Database Administrator

6. Networking Specialist

7. Information Technology Consultant

8. Web-consultant
