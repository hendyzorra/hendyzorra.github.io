THE STUDY PROGRAM OF
INFORMATION SYSTEM

The under graduate studies in Information System are designed to broaden graduates knowledge and provide a greater depth of understanding of their:

1. Integrity and identity as an Indonesian

2. Information System Development

3. Information System Department Process

4. Analysing problems critically to get the solution, organizing the plan and documenting it accordingly.

5. Accounting and management analysis

6. Continuing study


In general the available career for the under graduates studies in Information System are :

1. The Head of Information System Department

2. Project Manager of Information System

3. Program Analyst

4. Database Administrator

5. Public Accountant

6. Networking Specialist

7. Management Consultant of Information System
